# AI Customer Success Playbooks

This repository contains experiments exploring how AI can improve Customer Success workflows, customer insight generation, and value realisation.

I created this as part of my interest in how AI tools can help Customer Success teams operate more effectively in high-growth technology companies.

## Areas I'm exploring

- AI-assisted customer insight analysis
- Generating executive-ready customer summaries
- Identifying churn risks from customer signals
- Automating preparation for QBRs and value reviews

## Example AI workflows

### Customer Insight Analysis

Prompt example:

"Analyse the following customer conversation notes and identify potential churn risks, product feedback, and expansion opportunities."

### Executive Summary Generation

Prompt example:

"Summarise this customer activity into a short executive briefing highlighting adoption trends, key risks, and opportunities."

## Why this matters

As AI capabilities improve, Customer Success teams will increasingly rely on AI to analyse customer signals, surface insights, and help teams focus on the highest-impact actions.

This repository captures some early experiments in applying AI to Customer Success operations.
