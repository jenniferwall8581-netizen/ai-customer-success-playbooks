Customer Success AI Prompt Library

This document contains experiments exploring how AI can help Customer Success teams analyse customer signals, prepare executive updates, and identify risks or growth opportunities.

1. Churn Risk Detection Prompt

Purpose:
Identify potential churn risks from customer communications and usage signals.

Prompt
You are a Customer Success strategist.

Analyse the following customer information and identify potential churn risks.

Customer data may include:
- meeting notes
- support tickets
- product usage trends
- customer feedback

Please provide:

1. Key churn risk signals
2. Root causes behind the risks
3. Recommended actions for the Customer Success Manager
4. Opportunities to strengthen the relationship

Focus on actionable insights rather than generic advice.
